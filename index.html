Why athletes ?

Some French Olympic athletes come from low-income backgrounds and face financial challenges despite living in a developed country. In France, athletes who are not part of the national team receive limited financial support from the government, and they often have to rely on sponsorships and crowdfunding to cover their training and competition expenses. This can be particularly difficult for athletes who come from low-income households and do not have access to the same resources as their wealthier counterparts. Despite these challenges, many French Olympic athletes have succeeded in their respective sports and have represented their country at the highest level of competition. We want to ensure that this continues by helping professional athletes.
